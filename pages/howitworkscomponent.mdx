The Component Model is a digital representation of your datasheet.&#x20;

It contains information extracted from the PDF, organized into chapters for easy navigation.&#x20;

  <video autoPlay muted loop playsInline className="w-full aspect-video" src="https://static.embedd.it/img/Componnet_Model.mp4" />
    

You can edit all information, override any extracted data, and maintain full control over what goes into the code.

The Component Model serves as your single source of truth for the component, enabling you to generate multiple drivers and test environments for different targets.

For details about specific features, please refer to each section of the model.
