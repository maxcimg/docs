---
title: "Registering a profile "
---

In order to gain access to Embedd you will need to register a profile.

To register please go to:

[https://auth.embedd.it](https://auth.embedd.it)

Upon registration you will receive an email asking to confirm your email address. Please do so to obtain unobstructed access to the product.

You can access the product either via Web-based application or via VSCode Extension. Please refer to the next sections for detailed information.
