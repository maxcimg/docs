Don't use VSCode? You can still access all Embedd features through your browser.&#x20;

[**Web based platform**](https://platform.embedd.it)

Both the VSCode Extension and web-based application are built on VSCode and provide the same experience. Your account data is accessible through both platforms.

Please note that following browsers are **not supported**:

* Mozilla Firefox
* Safari
* Opera
