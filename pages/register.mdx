---
title: "Registering a profile "
---

To access Embedd, you'll need to register a profile.

Register at:[**https://auth.embedd.it/**](https://auth.embedd.it/)

After registering, you'll receive an email to confirm your address. Confirm it to get full access to the product.

You can use Embedd through either the web-based application or VSCode Extension. See the sections below for details.
